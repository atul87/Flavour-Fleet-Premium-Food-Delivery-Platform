## ✅ How to Use All Features

1. **Shopping Cart**: Click "Add" buttons on products to add them to cart
2. **Customization**: Click "Customize" button on any product to add toppings
3. **Pizza Builder**: Click "Build Your Own Pizza" button in the banner
4. **User Account**: Click user icon to register/login
5. **Language Toggle**: Use the HI switch in the header to toggle languages
6. **Search**: Use the search bar to find products
7. **Filtering**: Use category buttons to filter products
8. **Order Tracking**: Click "Track Your Order" button in delivery section
9. **View Cart**: Click cart icon to view cart contents
10. **Checkout**: Click "OK" in cart popup to checkout
